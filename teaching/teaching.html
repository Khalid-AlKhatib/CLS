Language Technology Project

In this project-based course, students are going to work on a natural language processing challenge. A challenge is a practical task in NLP related to a particular research topic.
Project Text Analysis

This is an introductory course to processing large amounts of textual data. The basics of language processing will be introduced both from a theoretical and practical perspectives.
