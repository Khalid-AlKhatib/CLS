Teaching

As a research group, we are committed to educating the next generation of scientists and engineers. Our members have taught a variety of courses, including:

Introduction to Computer Science

An introduction to the intellectual enterprises of computer science and the art of programming.
Data Structures and Algorithms

This is a rigorous course on the design and analysis of efficient algorithms and their associated data structures.
Introduction to Machine Learning

This program will constitute a non-technical introduction to Machine Learning (ML), with particular emphasis on their current applications in different fields
Advanced Machine Learning

This focuses on advanced machine learning topics using various platforms where you will get hands-on experience optimizing, deploying, and scaling ML models.
